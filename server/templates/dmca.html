DMCA / Takedown Policy

Last updated: April 2025

EZRA is an ephemeral, encrypted file transfer system. We do not view, store, or index user-uploaded content, and we are unable to decrypt files or determine their contents.

If you believe your copyrighted content has been made available via this service, please submit a takedown request including:

  • Your full legal name and contact information
  • A description of the work you believe is being infringed
  • The corresponding secret of the file you wish us to remove
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the information you provide is accurate

Please note that due to the encrypted and anonymous nature of this system, we may be unable to act on requests unless specific technical identifiers are provided.
