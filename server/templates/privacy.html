Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

No Logs. No Metadata. No Surveillance.

EZRA is designed from the ground up to preserve user anonymity and privacy. We do not collect or retain:

  • IP addresses
  • Login credentials or identifiers
  • Access logs or request history
  • File names or contents
  • Encryption keys

Client-Side Encryption

All files uploaded through EZRA are encrypted in the browser using strong AES-256-GCM encryption. The encryption key is never shared with the server. Even if compelled by legal order, we cannot decrypt your data because we never possess the decryption key.
