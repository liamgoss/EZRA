Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

EZRA is designed to operate without collecting or storing personal data. We do not log IP addresses, timestamps, or user activity.

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • IP addresses
  • Access logs
  • User identifiers or login credentials
  • File names or metadata

Ephemeral Storage

Files uploaded to EZRA are encrypted and stored temporarily. They are automatically deleted based on expiry or access rules. No backups are retained, and no persistent identifiers are associated with uploads.
